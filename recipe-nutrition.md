## Recipes

### Easy Veggie Wrap (Milo's version)
*Base: A Couple Cooks. Modifications: no carrots, no avocado, add hummus spread and a handful of fresh sprouts per wrap, chickpea filling yields 3 full wraps not 2.*

**Yield:** 3 wraps | **Serving:** 1 wrap

| Cal | Protein | Carbs | Fibre | Sat Fat | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~485 | ~28g | ~57g | ~12g | ~2.5g | ~545mg |

**Key ingredients per wrap (approximate split):**
- 1 high-protein tortilla (Dempster's High Protein, label-verified): 150 cal, 13g protein, 19g carbs, 4g fibre, 1g sat fat, 260mg sodium
- ⅓ of chickpea filling (PC Blue Menu 540mL can + avo oil mayo): ~197 cal, 8g protein, 24g carbs, 5g fibre, 1g sat fat, 70mg sodium
- Hummus (~¼ cup spread): ~110 cal, 4g protein, 10g carbs, 2g fibre, 0.5g sat fat, 200mg sodium
- Shredded red cabbage + iceberg lettuce: ~12 cal, 0.5g protein, 2g carbs, 0.5g fibre, 0g sat fat, 10mg sodium
- Fresh sprouts (handful, ~30g): ~15 cal, 2g protein, 1.5g carbs, 0.5g fibre, 0g sat fat, 5mg sodium

**Notes:**
- Chickpeas: PC Blue Menu no-salt-added 540mL can (label-verified: 110 cal / 6g protein per ½ cup)
- Tortilla: Dempster's High Protein (label-verified: 150 cal / 13g protein per wrap)
- Avocado oil mayo used (Chosen Foods or Primal Kitchen) in chickpea filling
- No carrots, no avocado
- Sprouts are homegrown Mumms seeds
- Adding sliced chicken breast boosts protein by ~35g and ~165 cal per wrap
- Very flexible — ingredients are suggestions per recipe feedback log
