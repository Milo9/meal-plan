## Recipes

### Easy Veggie Wrap (Milo's version)
*Base: A Couple Cooks. Modifications: no carrots, add a handful of fresh sprouts per wrap, chickpea filling yields 3 full wraps not 2.*

**Yield:** 3 wraps | **Serving:** 1 wrap

| Cal | Protein | Carbs | Fibre | Sat Fat | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ~415 | ~16g | ~47g | ~14g | ~3g | ~500mg |

**Key ingredients per wrap (approximate split):**
- 1 high-protein tortilla (Carb Balance or similar, 13g protein): ~180 cal, 13g protein, 22g carbs, 10g fibre, 1g sat fat, 270mg sodium
- ⅓ of chickpea filling (chickpeas, bell pepper, cucumber, avocado oil mayo, lemon, spices): ~175 cal, 3g protein, 22g carbs, 4g fibre, 2g sat fat, 200mg sodium
- Fresh sprouts (handful, ~30g): ~15 cal, 2g protein, 1.5g carbs, 0.5g fibre, 0g sat fat, 5mg sodium
- Avocado (¼ avocado per wrap): ~60 cal, 1g protein, 3g carbs, 2g fibre, 0.5g sat fat, 5mg sodium

**Notes:**
- Avocado oil mayo used (Chosen Foods or Primal Kitchen) — not regular mayo
- No carrots
- Sprouts are homegrown Mumms seeds
- Adding sliced chicken breast boosts protein by ~35g and ~165 cal per wrap
- Very flexible — ingredients are suggestions per recipe feedback log
